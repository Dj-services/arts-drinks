The Storyteller

Meet Thalisson

True Storymaker began with a simple belief: the moments that matter most deserve more than a passing snapshot. Every wedding toast, every quinceañera waltz, every corporate milestone carries a story worth telling with care.

Founder Thalisson built the studio around that idea — pairing cinematic videography with the energy of a great bar and the reach of social media, so every celebration is captured, poured, and shared with the same level of intention.

The result is a Boston-based creative studio trusted to show up prepared, move quietly through a room, and deliver work that feels as authentic as the moment itself.

— Thalisson Nunes, Founder
